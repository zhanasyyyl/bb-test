Test Overview

The digital SAT, PSAT/NMSQT, PSAT 10, and PSAT 8/9 each consist of a Reading and Writing section and a Math section.

Section 1: Reading and Writing (54 Questions)

There are two modules in the Reading and Writing section. Each module has 27 questions.

Section 2: Math (44 Questions)

There are two modules in the Math section. Each module has 22 questions.

Modules

Within each section, the two modules are timed separately. If time permits, you can review your answers in a given module. When the timer reaches zero, you will automatically move on. Once you move on from any module, you cannot return to it.
